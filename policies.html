Quick Loan Guide

Know your salary and company type

Check the minimum salary rules and whether your employer is accepted by the lender.

Find eligible lenders

Focus only on banks or NBFCs that match your salary and company category.

Compare interest rates

Look at the range offered by each lender. Lower rates mean cheaper loans.

Check special rules

Some lenders may restrict unlisted companies or have additional conditions.

Gather required documents

Prepare salary slips, ID proof, and employer details before applying.

Submit your application

The lender will review your credit score, salary, and existing liabilities.

Get final approval and rate

Meeting eligibility doesn’t guarantee approval; the lender decides the final terms.
